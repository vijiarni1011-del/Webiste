FAQ

Common questions from engineers

01What engineering calculations does the platform include?↓

BatteryMIS includes 22 interconnected engineering calculators covering: cell sizing and S x P configuration, pack energy and DoD, voltage window and string matrix, current and C-rate analysis, DCIR mapping by temperature and SoC, thermal management and TMS sizing, power map and drive cycle simulation, BMS threshold calculations, busbar resistance and derating, precharge and fuse sizing, lifecycle and SoH modelling, BOM and cost analysis, OCV curve analysis, 3D pack viewer and target vs results comparison. Every module feeds every other.

02How does the Standards AI search work?↓

Standards AI uses a RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline. Your standards PDFs are chunked with section-aware metadata and stored in a Chroma vector database using OpenAI embeddings. When you ask a question, the system uses 4-tier resolution - explicit section reference, dynamic index lookup, keyword fallback, then semantic search - to retrieve the most relevant clauses. GPT-4o answers using only the retrieved content with full clause citations. It never answers from training data.

03How does the AI analyse test data against targets?↓

Upload your test result files to the designated Google Drive folder and trigger the analysis webhook. BatteryMIS reads every measured value, compares it against your project acceptance criteria stored from the Engineering Suite, and generates a line-by-line pass/fail verdict. Deviations are flagged with the magnitude of exceedance. The final DVP and R report is generated automatically with all results, verdicts and risk flags.

04Can I add my own standards PDFs to the AI search?↓

Yes, but PDF ingestion is managed by the BatteryMIS team during your onboarding. If you have specific standards you need indexed, please reach out via the Request Access form and mention the standard families you work with. We will set up your library as part of the access provisioning. You can also add standard names to your indexed list directly in the Standards AI setup panel once you have access.

05What vehicle types does the DVP automation support?↓

BatteryMIS supports all EV applications - passenger cars, buses, trucks, 2-wheelers, e-bikes and off-highway vehicles including excavators, wheel loaders and agricultural equipment. The DVP automation applies the correct standards family for each vehicle class and voltage category automatically, ensuring regulatory compliance across markets including EU, US, China, India and Japan.
